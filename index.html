Privacy Policy – VeNoM IPTV

Last updated: January 2026

VeNoM IPTV respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use our application.

1. Information Collection

VeNoM IPTV does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

2. Device Information

The application may access basic device information required for proper functionality, such as network connectivity status. This information is not stored or shared.

3. Advertisements

The free version of the application may display advertisements provided by third-party ad services (e.g. Google AdMob). These services may collect anonymous usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Users who purchase the Remove Ads option will no longer see advertisements.

4. In-App Purchases

VeNoM IPTV offers a one-time in-app purchase to remove advertisements. All purchase processing is handled securely by Google Play. The application does not store or process payment information.

5. Children’s Privacy

VeNoM IPTV does not knowingly collect any personal data from children under the age of 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
